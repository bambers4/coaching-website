The Process

How online coaching works

Everything is done remotely. You don't need to be local. Here's what working together looks like from start to finish.

1

Initial Conversation

We start with a short message or call about your goals, current fitness, schedule, and what's worked for you before.

2

Personalized Training Plan

I build a plan tailored to your goal, your available days, and your current fitness — not a generic template.

3

Weekly Feedback

You train, I stay available. Regular check-ins, feedback on your runs, and plan adjustments whenever life changes.

4

Race Preparation

As your goal race approaches, we dial in the taper, race pace, and race-day strategy so you arrive ready to run well.

What You'll Get

What working together looks like

📅 A weekly training schedule

Structured runs with a clear purpose each day — easy runs, workouts, long runs, and rest. No guessing what to do.

💬 Regular communication

You can always reach me with questions, run updates, or to flag if something feels off or isn't working.

🔄 Plan adjustments

Life happens. If you miss a week, get sick, or feel beat up, we adapt the plan. We don't just push through blindly.

📊 Progress over time

We track your improvement week to week so you can see and feel how far you've come since day one.

🧠 Education along the way

I'll share the reasoning behind your training — pacing, easy effort, fueling, and other fundamentals — so you become a smarter runner.

🎯 Honest feedback

I'll tell you what's working, what needs work, and how to keep improving. No vague encouragement — real, useful input.

Not sure where to start?

Just reach out. Tell me a little about your goals and we'll find the right fit. No commitment required.
