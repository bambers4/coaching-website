Introduction

Hi, I'm Michael

I'm a former NCAA D1 cross country and track & field athlete based in Sacramento, CA. I've been running competitively since 2014, and over the past 13+ years I've experienced just about every part of the sport — the big breakthroughs, the setbacks, the grind of back-to-back training blocks, and the feeling of crossing a finish line knowing you gave everything you had.

I started coaching because I wanted to share what that journey taught me. I believe every runner — regardless of pace or experience — deserves a plan that actually fits their life and a coach who's genuinely invested in their progress.

What I Believe

My Coaching Philosophy

A few principles that guide everything I do as a coach.

Consistency beats intensity

The best training plan is one you can actually stick to. Showing up consistently over weeks and months matters far more than any single hard workout.

The athlete always comes first

Every runner I work with has different goals, a different schedule, and a different body. Plans get adjusted to fit you — not the other way around.

Progress takes patience

Building real fitness takes time, and that's okay. We celebrate small wins along the way because each one is a step in the right direction.

Communication matters

Good coaching is a two-way conversation. I want to hear how training is going so we can make smart decisions together.

Want to work together?

Send me a message and let's figure out if we're a good fit. No pressure — just a conversation.
