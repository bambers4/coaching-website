My Coaching Philosophy

Coaching that fits your needs

13+ years of competitive running has shown me that training isn't one-size-fits-all. I design your training plan by understanding where you are, where you want to go, and building a realistic plan to get you there.

🎯

Goal-Driven Plans

Every plan starts with your goal. We work backward from the finish line.

▪

Structured Training

Weekly plans that build progressively over a training block to help you be fit, healthy, and confident on the starting line.

💬

Ongoing Support

With me, you'll never train alone. Regular check-ins, feedback on your runs, and adjustments to your training schedule when life gets in the way.

Who I Work With

Built for runners at every starting line

I work with runners of all backgrounds and experience levels. If you're motivated to improve and willing to put in the work, we're a good fit.

  • Complete beginners who have never run before
  • Runners returning after a break or injury
  • 5K and 10K racers chasing a personal best
  • First-time half marathon and marathon runners
  • High school or college athletes looking for structure
  • Anyone who wants to run consistently and feel good doing it

About Me

Coaching built on real experience

I'm Michael Chambers, a former NCAA D1 XC and T&F athlete based in Sacramento, CA. I've been running since 2014 and have competed in everything from local 5Ks to collegiate cross country and track races.

My coaching approach is shaped by my own athletic journey; what I've tried, seen, and learned along the way.
