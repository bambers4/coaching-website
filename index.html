My Approach

Simple, consistent, sustainable

I keep training straightforward. The goal is to help you build a habit you enjoy, run without getting hurt, and improve steadily over time.

Consistency over intensity

Showing up regularly matters more than any single hard workout. We build habits first, then performance follows.

Plans that fit real life

Training needs to work around your schedule, not the other way around. If something changes, we adapt.

Communication that helps

You can always ask questions. I'll give you honest, clear answers — not jargon-heavy explanations.

Long-term thinking

The goal isn't just your next race — it's building a running practice you'll maintain for years to come.
